Biography

I'm currently a third-year Ph.D. student at MMLab@NTU, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. Previously, I received my M.Eng. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology and B.Eng. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. I'm fortunate to have internship at SenseTime Research. I'm broadly interested in the field of computational photography and computer vision. My current research focuses on image/video restoration and generation.
